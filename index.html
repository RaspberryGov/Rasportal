OUR PLEDGE

Sustenance, Community, Knowledge.

Freeberry is a collective of like-minded individuals dedicated to the principle of living sustainably off the land. We reject commercial exploitation of natural resources, focusing instead on sharing ancestral and practical knowledge to ensure self-sufficiency and communal well-being.

  • ♦
    Our collective members share crucial knowledge on foraging, sustainable harvesting, crafts, and natural building.
  • ♦
    Stewards of the Land focus on utilizing resources mindfully and locally, ensuring the land remains abundant for future generations.
THE FREEBERRY ECONOMY

$BERRIES: A Token of Trust and Participation

Tokenized Currency

$BERRIES is our utility token, built on the TRON Blockchain. It was distributed **for free** to the founding Freeberry First Nations tribe members to kickstart the economy.

Open Exchange

The public is welcomed! Engage in the Freeberry economy by offering your goods, services, or expertise in exchange for $BERRIES.

15% Annual Rewards

Lock your $BERRIES to participate in the staking mechanism and earn 15% Universal Rewards for supporting the long-term stability of the economy.

WHERE WE ARE

From South Head to the Global Web

The physical heart of the Freeberry economy is situated in the South Head. This is where our community actively demonstrates self-sufficiency.

While our physical roots are deep, our economy is not bound by borders. Free-trade is encouraged anywhere individuals can exchange goods, services, and barter using $BERRIES.
