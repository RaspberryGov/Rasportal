Review the F.A.Q.'s and get comfortable with The Free Nation of Raspberry

What is The Free Nation of Raspberry?
The Free Nation of Raspberry is a decentralized and sovereign micronation comprised of global citizens who advocate for free speech, fiscal autonomy, self-governance, and the right to livelihood, grounded in a respect for the Earth's natural resources. The nation's ethos emphasizes the sustainable utilization of land and resources to foster widespread prosperity and ensure basic accessibility for all inhabitants and travelers, rather than promoting physical ownership or expansive enterprise development.
What is Raspberry Dollar (RBD)?
The Raspberry Dollar (RBD) serves as the official digital currency within the Free Nation of Raspberry ecosystem. This open-source digital currency, developed by The Comptroller; Digital Currency Daily, operates on the TRON Blockchain, allowing for public access, modification, and redistribution of its codebase. The value of RBD is determined solely by user consensus within its ecosystem, possessing no inherent external value. Developers are encouraged to create applications that integrate with the Raspberry Dollar smart contract on the TRON Blockchain, thereby fostering the expansion of its utility and the ecosystem's economic growth.

What is the Universal Basic Income (UBI)?
The Universal Basic Income is the hard-coded inflation mechanism for the Raspberry Dollar. The UBI mints RBD at a rate of 15% annually based on the deposited amount.
What is the Raspberry Passport?
The Raspberry Passport is an official Non-Fungible Token (NFT) offered to supporters of The Free Nation of Raspberry. This digital document provides holders with a designated allocation of delegated energy and bandwidth on the TRON network, ensuring a limited quantity of complimentary transactions from the associated TRON wallet. The benefits provided by acquiring a passport are non-transferrable.
Why do people support The Raspberry Dollar as money in The Free Nation of Raspberry?
Supporters of The Free Nation of Raspberry movement adopt this open-source software as a medium of exchange due to its equitable distribution model, exclusivity to citizens, and its transparent, inherently limited, and consistently predictable inflationary framework. These individuals possess an understanding of monetary principles and consciously choose to transact using Raspberry Dollars for goods and services within their community.
How do I get Raspberry Dollars (RBD)?
Inside RAS Portal on the RBD/TRX app, you can exchange TRX to RBD seamlessly using buy/sell functions embedded in the smart contract. The exchange rate is 1000 RBD per 100 TRX, with a floating tariff fee applied to the conversion rate based upon market demands. When you exchange TRX to RBD using our app, the TRX will remain in the token smart contract, growing the value of our smart contract economy, while also allowing you to exchange back to TRX,should you choose to exit the economy at a later date.
How can I earn Raspberry Dollars (RBD)?
You can earn Raspberry Dollars by participating in the economy by providing goods and services to fellow community members and accept RBD in return. Additionally, you can earn Raspberry Dollars daily from the Universal Basic Income app, Simply acquire RBD or contribute your airdrop allocation to the UBI and earn 15% annually. These methods facilitate the circulation and utility of the currency within the ecosystem.
Is there a tax to use raspberry dollars?
The Raspberry Dollar carries no inherent transaction fees or taxation within its ecosystem. However, it's important to note that the underlying TRON Blockchain levies transactional fees for interacting with smart contract functions, including the transfer of TRC-20 tokens like the Raspberry Dollar. Users will therefore require TRX to cover these network costs, or sufficient energy and bandwidth allocations in their wallet to perform transactions without direct TRX expenditure. Acquiring a Raspberry Passport can help offset these costs as a citizen.
What is a Tariff?
A tariff is a floating fee applied to the exchange rate of RBD, based on market demands to help grow the value of the economy. The tariff increases/decreases by 0.1% per 100 TRX exchanged in/out of the smart contract economy. The Tariff is not collected by any entity.
How do I store Raspberry Dollars?
You can store raspberry dollars in any TRON based wallet, such as TronLink.
