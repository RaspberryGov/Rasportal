What is The Free Nation of Raspberry?
The Free Nation of Raspberry is a decentralized, free and sovereign micronation of global citizens, who believe in free speech, freedom of taxation, self-governance over bureaucratic government, the right to access livelihood, and who respect the way of the land as native inhabitants to Planet Earth.

We do not believe in physical possession of the planet or development of enterprise, but rather utilization of the land and its resources to spread prosperity and accessibility of a basic livelihood to those who reside upon it and travel amongst it.
What is Raspberry Dollar (RBD)?
The Raspberry Dollar is the trusted money utilized within The Free Nation of Raspberry ecosystem by its citizens and visitors to barter goods and services. It is open source code that creates decentralized software hosted on the TRON Blockchain, designed and released by The Comptroller; Digital Currency Daily.

The code base for the open source software that brings the raspberry dollar into fruition to be used as money is publicly accessible on the blockchain and can be copied, replicated, modified and redistributed by anyone who wishes to remodel the software code for their own use for a variety of purposes. It has no inherent value or return value for any other currency outside of the individual beliefs agreed upon by those who utilize it in their daily activities.

Anyone that wishes to interact with the raspberry dollar software code on TRON Blockchain being used as money within the free nation of raspberry is free to program, design and release their own software or applications that communicate with the raspberry dollar smart contract hosted within the TRON blockchain web library to grow the economy and create additional uses.

What is the inflation rate of the Raspberry Dollars (RBD)?
The Inflation rate is permanently coded into the token software at 15% annually. This means if you hold 10 Raspberry Dollars, you can potentially generate an additional 1.5 RBD per year to use within the economy, if utilizing the staking mechanism for 365 consecutive days.
What is the Raspberry Passport?
The Raspberry Passport is an official NFT document available for supporters of the Free Nation of Raspberry. The passport grants purchasers with an allocation of delegated energy/bandwidth to ensure a finite amount of free transaction availability from the corrosponding TRON wallet.
Why do people support The Raspberry Dollar as money in The Free Nation of Raspberry?
Enthusiasts of the Free Nation of Raspberry movement choose to embrace this open source software as a money/currency due to its fair distribution model to citizens only, and permanent inflation structure which is predictable, fair, and scarcely limited by nature.

Enthusiasts understand the properties of money and why they choose to exchange Raspberry Dollars as such to barter for goods and services amongst others within our community.
How do I get Raspberry Dollars (RBD)?
The Raspberry Dollar was initially distributed via free airdrop to supporting members of the free nation. There has been an allocation of tokens deposited as liquidity on Sunswap for public access to the token, which trades at a free market exchange rate for TRX.
How do I earn Raspberry Dollars (RBD)?
As within any economy, to earn raspberry currency you can provide goods and services to other community members and accept Raspberry Dollars as payment. Alternatively, you can acquire it or “stake” your initial airdrop allocation of Raspberry Dollars and earn new raspberry dollars each day to spend within the economy.
Is there a tax to use raspberry dollars?
No, there is no taxation to use the Raspberry Dollar, in any form.

However, the TRON Blockchain charges transactional fees to interact with smart contract functions, such as sending and receiving currency-like trc-20 tokens on the TRON blockchain so you will need TRX to perform transactions, or increased energy/bandwidth allocations on your wallet to perform transactions without cost.
How do I store Raspberry Dollars?
You can store raspberry dollars in any TRON based wallet, such as TronLink.
